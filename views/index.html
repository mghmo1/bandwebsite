BAND'S HISTORY
  • founded in 1987
  • 1988: Kurt smashes his first guitar
  • June, 1989: Release of Bleach
  • Brief England Tour (1990)
  • Signing at DGC Records
  • 1991: Nevermind is released
  • Six-week European Tour
  • 1992: Tour through Australia, Japan & Hawaii
  • 1993: Release of In Utero
  • Three-month North-America Tour
  • 8 April, 1994: Shotgun Suicide of Kurt Cobain
  • 2002: "You know you're right" is released. Last song Kurt, Krist and Dave ever recorded.
  • 2009: 20th Anniversary version of Nirvana's debut album is released.
